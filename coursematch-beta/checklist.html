üìÖ Early Preparation Start Here

  • Check the official Course Match calendar
    Visit CBS website to confirm your ranking window dates and deadlines
  • Check for registration holds
    Visit Student Services. Common holds: immunization, financial, academic advising
  • Meet with your academic advisor
    Discuss degree requirements, recommended courses, and your academic plan
  • Review course offerings
    Browse course catalog, check meeting times, read descriptions
  • Check prerequisites
    Ensure you meet requirements for courses you're interested in
  • Research professors
    Check ratings, teaching styles, and course workload

üìù Before Ranking Opens Prepare

  • Determine your target credits
    Remember: Use 1.5 increments (12.0, 13.5, 15.0, etc.)
  • Create your course list
    List 2x your target credits worth of courses (30 credits if taking 15)
  • Check time conflicts
    Map out potential schedule conflicts between courses
  • Identify backup courses
    Have "safety" options you'd be satisfied with
  • Review historical data
    Check which courses were highly competitive in past terms

üí° Planning Tips

  • Mix core requirements with electives
  • Consider workload balance across the week
  • Include courses with different assessment styles
  • Don't rank more than 25 courses total

üöÄ When Ranking Opens Action Time

  • Use Chrome or Firefox browser
    NOT Safari - it has compatibility issues
  • Log in to coursematch.gsb.columbia.edu
    Use your UNI and password
  • Set your target credits
    Must be in 1.5 increments (e.g., 12.0, 13.5, 15.0)
  • Add courses to your selection
    Search and add ~2x your target credits worth
  • Rank courses in buckets
    Favorite (1 only), Great, Good, Acceptable
  • Order within each bucket
    Drag to arrange preferences within buckets
  • Review preview schedules
    Check the 8 possible schedules shown
  • Add Rules if needed
    For substitute courses (e.g., multiple sections)

‚è∞ 24 Hours Before Deadline Critical

  • SUBMIT YOUR RANKINGS
    Don't wait until the last minute! System can get overloaded
  • Screenshot your final rankings
    Keep a record of what you submitted
  • Double-check bucket assignments
    Ensure courses are in the right priority buckets
  • Verify submission confirmation
    Look for the confirmation message/email

üö® Last-Minute Reminders

  • Deadline is 4:00 PM EST (not midnight!)
  • You can change rankings unlimited times until deadline
  • Only your final submission counts
  • System may be slow near deadline - submit early!

üìä After Results Are Released Follow-up

  • Check your assigned schedule
    Results typically released 24-48 hours after deadline
  • Join waitlists strategically
    CBS typically allows max 3 waitlists
  • Attend ALL first classes
    Including waitlisted courses - professors often add students
  • Prepare for Add/Drop
    Have backup plans ready for the 2-week Add/Drop period
